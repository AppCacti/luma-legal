Getting Started

How do I log a feed?

Tap the left or right breast button on the main screen to start a timer. Tap it again to stop — the session is saved automatically with the time, side, and duration.

For bottle or pump sessions, tap the Bottle/Pump tab at the bottom of the screen.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. Luma works fully as a guest — all your data is stored locally on your device.

Optional: tap Settings → Sign In with Apple if you want to link an account (useful for account deletion or future sync features).

Where is my data stored?

All feeding logs are stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is sent to any server unless you sign in with Apple (which only shares your account ID, not your feeding data).

Apple Watch

How do I use Luma on Apple Watch?

Install the Luma Watch app from the Watch app on your iPhone. Once installed, open Luma on your Watch and use the same left/right tap to log sessions.

Data syncs directly between your iPhone and Watch — no internet connection needed.

The Watch app isn't syncing — what do I do?

Make sure both apps are open. Watch Connectivity works best when both the iPhone and Watch apps are in the foreground. Try force-quitting and reopening both apps if sync seems stuck.

Subscriptions & Luma Pro

What's included in Luma Pro?

Luma Pro unlocks detailed history charts, advanced insights, custom reminders, and the Breastfeeding Guide. The free version includes unlimited feed logging.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find Luma, and tap Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

You cannot cancel through the app itself — this is managed by Apple.

I paid for Pro but it's not showing — how do I restore?

Tap the Restore Purchases button on the Luma Pro paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund on your purchase.

Privacy & Data

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes your account from our servers.

To delete your locally stored feeding logs, tap Settings → Delete All Data.

You can also email us at empire@tan.wiki to request manual deletion.

Do you share my data with anyone?

No. We never sell or share your feeding data. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

The app crashed or something isn't working

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Luma, then offload and reinstall the app (your data is preserved).

If it's still broken, email us at empire@tan.wiki with a description of what happened and what device/iOS version you're on.

I forgot to stop a timer — can I edit it?

Yes — tap on any session in your History to edit the start time, end time, and side. Changes are saved immediately.
